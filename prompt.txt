Create a fully playable, single‑file web game titled "Attack on Tide" using HTML5 Canvas, CSS, and JavaScript.
Output: ONE SINGLE index.html file containing everything.

================================================================================
GAME OVERVIEW
================================================================================
- Genre: lane‑defence, inspired by Plants vs. Zombies.
- 5 lanes × 10 grid columns.
- Defend your farm from waves of historically themed soldiers.
- Two resources: Buns (steamed buns) and Blue Sacred Flames.
- 8 hero units, 5 power‑up cards, last‑resort Spike Rollers.
- 6 eras/stages, all unlocked from the start.
- Strict M.U.G.E.N. art style for all characters.

================================================================================
GRID, LANES, AND POSITIONING
================================================================================
- 5 horizontal lanes, each with 10 grid slots (columns 1 to 10).
- Column 1 = leftmost (player base), column 10 = rightmost (enemy spawn).
- Enemies move right to left.
- A Spike Roller sits in column 1 of every lane.
- Player units can be placed only on empty cells in columns 2–10.
- CRITICAL PLACEMENT RULE: The game must NOT prevent placing a unit on a cell just because an enemy is in the lane ahead. Only block placement if the exact cell is occupied by an enemy or another unit. (Like PvZ: you can place a plant right in front of a zombie.)
- EXCEPTION for Spikeweed: Spikeweed CAN be placed on a cell currently occupied by an enemy. It triggers instantly, killing that enemy and then disappearing.

================================================================================
RESOURCES
================================================================================
1. Buns (steamed buns)
   - Start: 50 buns.
   - Earned by Farmers (see units) – every 10 sec a floating bun appears above a random lane; click to collect, vanishes after 5 sec.
   - Spent on hero units.

2. Blue Sacred Flames
   - Start: 0.
   - Earned by defeating any UNIQUE enemy (i.e., not basic Bronze/Silver/Gold/Star/BambooJumper/SwordShield). Each unique enemy drops a blue flame orb worth 50 flames, floating for 5 sec, click to collect.
   - Spent on power‑up cards.

================================================================================
PLAYER UNITS (Heroes)
================================================================================
All placed from a card deck onto a grid cell. Cooldown is global per card.

  Farmer          | Cost 0   | CD 5s  | Resource generator: 25 buns every 10s. No attack. Available instantly at stage start (no initial cooldown).
  Shield Bearer   | Cost 50  | CD 5s  | Very high HP. Blocks movement; enemies must destroy it.
  Single Archer   | Cost 100 | CD 5s  | Fires one arrow per 1.5s down lane, medium damage.
  Double Archer   | Cost 200 | CD 5s  | Fires two arrows per 1.5s, medium damage each.
  Poison Dagger   | Cost 175 | CD 5s  | Throws dagger every 2s; slows target 3s, reveals invisible enemies 4s, low damage.
  Spikeweed Trap  | Cost 100 | CD 15s | Hidden trap. Instant‑kills first enemy that steps on it (or placed on it). If enemy is Female Rider (see enemies), removes bike, no crash damage. Trap disappears after activation.
  Mine            | Cost 25  | CD 25s | Takes 15s to arm (visual: small mound → red dot). Armed mine instantly kills first enemy that walks over it.
  Explosion       | Cost 150 | CD 40s | Cherry Bomb. Detonates after 0.8s, killing ALL enemies in a 3×3 area (3 columns wide × 3 lanes tall, centred on placement cell).

Placement rules: only empty cells (except Spikeweed can be placed on enemy). After first Farmer placed, all other cards become available. Cooldowns shown on cards.

================================================================================
POWER‑UP CARDS
================================================================================
Activated with Blue Sacred Flames. No buns needed. Each has its own cooldown.

  Purple Thunder    | Cost 100 flames | CD 30s | Instantly kills all enemies (lightning).
  Extinguish Fire   | Cost 100 flames | CD 30s | Instantly kills all enemies (water wave).
  Ice               | Cost 100 flames | CD 30s | Freezes all enemies 0.5s, then shatters them.
  The Chains        | Cost 50 flames  | CD 20s | Immobilises ALL enemies on screen for 5 seconds.
  Pushing Hand      | Cost 50 flames  | CD 20s | Pushes ALL enemies back to column 10.

All five cards are purple with distinct icons. Cooldowns displayed.

================================================================================
SPIKE ROLLER (Last‑Resort)
================================================================================
- Each lane has a Spike Roller in column 1.
- If an enemy reaches column 1, roller activates: rolls right, kills all enemies in that lane, then destroyed for rest of stage.
- If enemy reaches column 1 and roller is already used, lose 1 life (start with 3 lives). That enemy removed.

================================================================================
WAVES, DIFFICULTY, AND PREPARATION TIME
================================================================================
- 30‑SECOND PREPARATION: After stage starts, first enemy wave does NOT spawn for exactly 30 seconds. No visual countdown is shown; the player simply has 30 seconds to place units freely.
- After 30s, normal wave logic begins.
- Difficulty: Easy = 1 Major Wave, Medium = 2, Hard = 3.
- Major Waves end with a Star Soldier boss.
- Wave composition:
  * First waves: Bronze Soldiers only.
  * Later waves add tougher basics and current‑era special enemies.
  * On Medium/Hard, Silver/Gold appear much earlier.
  * Each wave has more enemies and tougher ratio than previous.
- Previous‑era specials can appear in current stage with a chance:
  * Easy: 15%, Medium: 30%, Hard: 45% per wave.
  * If triggered, one random special from any previous era is added to the wave.

================================================================================
ENEMY TYPES (ALL MUST BE M.U.G.E.N. STYLE)
================================================================================
All enemies: chibi, NO eyes, simple open mouth, walking animation (min 4 frames).
Death animation: face‑down, lying flat, then fade.

--- BASIC SOLDIERS (appear in every era) ---
  Bronze Soldier (spear)    - Low toughness
  Silver Soldier (spear)    - Medium toughness, slightly faster
  Gold Soldier (spear)      - High toughness
  Star Soldier (boss)       - Very high toughness, red flag on helmet. Only in Major Waves.
  Silver Bamboo Jumper      - Available from Stage 2. Jumps over first unit encountered (blocked by Shield Bearer). Holds bamboo pole.
  Gold Sword & Shield       - Available from Stage 2. Extremely high toughness, blocks 30% front damage.

  SPEAR HOLD: All spear soldiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Star) must hold the spear HORIZONTALLY with both hands, across the body.

--- ERA‑SPECIAL ENEMIES ---
Stage 1 – Tensei Era: no special enemies.

Stage 2 – Greek Era:
  Wrestler – Runs fast, then stops in lane when adjacent to a player unit. Stays there as obstacle. High HP.
  Mantyr Queen – Drains 2 buns/sec while alive. Low toughness.

Stage 3 – Medieval Era:
  Cannon – Spawns at col10, immobile. Fires cannonball every 5s, destroys first unit hit.
  BlakeMan – Fast speed, medium toughness, melee damage.

Stage 4 – Warfare Era:
  Ninja – Invisible. Revealed by Poison Dagger (4s). Upon reveal, spawns 2 new invisible Ninjas in random other lanes. Low HP.
  Samurai – Extremely high toughness, dual blades, melee.
  Chinese Martial Girl – Red qipao, 65% dodge against targeted attacks.

Stage 5 – Robotic Era:
  Sentinel – Occupies 2 cells, slow, attacks units in own and adjacent lanes. Very tough. Cannot be insta‑killed by Spikeweed/Explosion (takes double dmg instead).
  Female Rider – Motorcycle, very fast. Crashes at first unit/col1, dealing 3×3 area damage. Then walks normally (medium toughness). Spikeweed removes bike instantly, no crash.

Stage 6 – Hybrid Era:
  Underworld Dog – Red dog‑like soldier, hopping animation, medium toughness.
  Beautiful Female Witch – Flies over all ground units. Archers/Daggers can hit her. Medium toughness.
  Dragon – Two‑legged, walks. Extremely tough (Sentinel level). Spills fire (damage) or ice (slow) in its lane every 6s.
