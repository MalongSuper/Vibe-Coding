How to Play

Goal

The board starts completely filled with the wrong digits in the wrong places. Slide numbers between adjacent cells until every row, column, and block contains each digit exactly once.

Controls

Click or tap a cell to select it. Then use the arrow keys or WASD to swap its digit with the neighbouring cell in that direction. Only up, down, left, and right moves are allowed — no diagonals.

Highlights

  • The selected cell glows light orange.
  • Cells already matching the solved board glow light navy blue.

Info panels

The move counter sits to the left of the board and the timer to the right. Both start the moment you make your first swap.

Winning

Once every cell lands in its correct position, the puzzle is solved and your final time and move count are shown.
