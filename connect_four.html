How to Play

Connect Four is a two-player game of dropping discs into a standing grid.

  1. 1Take turns clicking or tapping a column to drop your disc into the lowest open slot.
  2. 2Player 1 plays garnet red and always opens the table.
  3. 3Be first to line up four discs in a row — across, up-and-down, or diagonally — to win.
  4. 4If all 42 slots fill with no line of four, the table ends in a draw.
