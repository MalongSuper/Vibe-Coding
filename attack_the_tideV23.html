Attack The Tide

Defend the farm through six eras with buns, blue sacred flames, and a very stubborn line of heroes.

How to Play

Click a hero card, then click an empty grid cell in columns 2-10 to place it. Column 1 belongs to Spike Rollers.

Farmers make floating buns every 10 seconds. Click buns before they vanish to gain 25 buns.

Special enemies drop blue sacred flame orbs. Click the orbs before they fade, then spend flames on Super Power cards.

Enemies move from the right. If one reaches column 1, that lane's Spike Roller clears the lane once. After that, leaks cost a life. Lose all 3 lives and the tide wins.

Survive every wave to unlock the next era.
