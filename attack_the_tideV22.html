Battle Rules

Objective: Hold out against waves of invaders moving down the combat lanes. If any enemy passes your final defense defenses on the far left, the tide will crush your encampment.


Placing Defenders: Spend 🥖 Buns collected over time to place units down onto any empty cell within columns 2 through 10. Placement is permitted even if an enemy is in the lane, provided the cell itself is empty.


Spikeweed Exception: Spikeweed can be positioned directly underneath active enemies to instantly damage them on the ground.


Removal Mode: Press the ❌ Remove Unit action button in the top right to switch into demolition mode. Click any active defender on the grid to clear the space instantly (no refunds). Press Escape or click again to cancel.


Blue Sacred Flames: Special glowing commanders drop Blue Sacred Flames upon defeat. Use them to deploy advanced hero elements and tactical features.
