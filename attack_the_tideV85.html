How to Play

⚔️ Place units – click a hero card, then click an empty cell (columns 2–12). Column 1 holds Spike Rollers.

🍞 Farmers produce buns at their location every 10s. Buns also fall from the sky every 20s. Click buns to gain 25 buns.

🔥 Sacred flames fall from the sky every 30s (click for 25 flames).

⚡ Super Powers – Thunder, Fire, Ice, Chains, Pushing Hand – affect the entire battlefield instantly. Click a power to use it.

💀 One breach – if any enemy reaches column 1 after the Spike Roller is gone, you lose instantly.

🏆 Survive every wave to unlock the next era.

⭐ Star Soldiers appear in major waves. Defeating one drops a star when defeated.
