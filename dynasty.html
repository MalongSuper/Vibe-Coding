Dynasty Defense

How to Play

Objective: Defend the central Emperor's Palace from 4 converging paths of enemy stickmen forces. Your remaining Palace Lives are visible beneath the fortress.

Strategic Deployment: When you have enough gold, units in the bottom right panel will illuminate. Select a configuration, then tap an available blue-highlighted plot to deploy.

Leveling Up: Select the same unit type and place it onto a standard unit you have already deployed to level it up (up to Level 5) for increased stats! (Heroes cannot level up).

Management: Click the "Demolish Coin" at the top right at any time to enter Sell Mode, then click any active red-highlighted unit to dismantle them.

Standard Units (50G): Blade (Fast), Archer (Long Range), Magic (High DMG), Mace (Slow/Heavy), Spear (Balanced).

Heroes (150G): Advanced commanders featuring dynamic battle configurations. Only one of each drafted hero can be deployed simultaneously.
